About Matt

A Pacific Northwest
photographer with
dirt on his boots.

Matt Demke grew up drawn to places that take some effort to reach — the kind where the light is worth waiting for and the silence says something. He shoots landscape, portrait, and film photography from his base in the Seattle area, and takes on commercial and editorial work for clients who want images with real feeling in them.

No presets. No shortcuts. Just time, light, and a genuine obsession with getting it right.
